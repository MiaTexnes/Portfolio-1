About Me

Hello! I'm a 34-year-old mother of two beautiful girls, living in the vibrant city of Oslo with my wonderful family. By day, I work full-time as a money counter and cash management systems technician, ensuring everything runs smoothly in the world of finance.


In my spare time, I'm a passionate student of front-end development at Noroff, where I'm diving into the exciting world of coding and design. My dream is to become proficient in these skills and eventually work in a field that combines my love for creativity and technology.


When I'm not buried in code, you can find me indulging in my hobbies. I absolutely love sewing, creating beautiful pieces with my hands. I'm also a big fan of movies and series, always on the lookout for the next great story to get lost in. And let's not forget my love for red wine – there's nothing quite like relaxing with a good glass of wine after a long day.


I'm an animal lover at heart, and while I adore all animals, I currently have a lovely collection of fishes that bring a touch of tranquility to my home.


Join me on my journey as I balance family life, work, and my passion for front-end development. I'm excited to see where this path takes me and to share my experiences along the way!
